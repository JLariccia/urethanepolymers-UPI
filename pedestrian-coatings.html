Acrylic Systems

UPI manufactures and distributes acrylic pedestrian coating systems that offer facility managers and design professionals an environmentally friendly alternative for their waterproofing solutions. These easy-to-install, low-odor systems provide a durable, protective barrier over concrete and plywood surfaces, helping to preserve the longevity of the structure.

Excel-Coat® Fire

The Excel-Coat Pedestrian Traffic Membrane is a fiberglass-reinforced, acrylic deck coating system designed to waterproof deck surfaces exposed to pedestrian traffic. This system is a four-step process composed of primer, a fiberglass-reinforced base coat, skid resistant texture coat and an acrylic sealer.

Excel-Coat® Decorative

Decorative protection for concrete and finishes for waterproofing systems. The Excel-Coat K/D System is a decorative latex modified cementitious coating that includes a base coat and texture coat, topped with an acrylic sealer.

Full System Description

The Excel-Coat K/D System can also be used in conjunction with the Excel-Coat waterproofing membranes. This combination provides unbeatable waterproof protection with infinite decorative possibilities.

The Excel-Coat K/D II System uses the same materials along with stencils, taping techniques, concrete skins, and stamps to provide more decorative finish options over concrete and our Fire Rated System.

Urethane Systems

As a leading manufacturer of polyurethane deck coating systems, UPI has developed some of the industry's toughest pedestrian traffic bearing coating systems. UPI high-performance and heavy-duty wear resistant systems form a seamless watertight barrier over concrete and plywood surfaces which protects the substrate against premature deterioration caused by moisture penetration.

Tufflex "Tuff" Pedestrian

This systems are seamless, provide UV and weather protection, prevent deterioration of plywood or concrete, stop scaling and spalling of concrete, reduce maintenance costs, and provide skid resistant surfacing.

This system utilizes single component, moisture cured elastomeric urethane waterproofing system designed for pedestrian traffic. It is a monolithic system, designed to waterproof concrete and plywood surfaces by excluding moisture penetration during low temperature exposure as well as high temperature, high humidity thermal cycling.

This system can be applied over sound concrete or sound wood sub-surfaces.
